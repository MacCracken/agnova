# Code of Conduct

agnova adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) verbatim.

In short: be respectful, be patient, be helpful. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to **robert.maccracken@gmail.com** with subject prefix `[agnova conduct]`. Reports are confidential and reviewed within 7 days.
